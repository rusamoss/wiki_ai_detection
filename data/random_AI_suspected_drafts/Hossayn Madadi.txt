Hossayn Madadi Persian: حسین مددی (born 1952) is an Iranian former footballer who played as a forward for Taj Shiraz, Taj Tehran (now Esteghlal F.C.), and Bargh Shiraz during the Takht Jamshid Cup era. He was part of the generation of Shiraz based players active in Iran’s top tier club football in the late 1960s and 1970s and is listed among veteran footballers of Shiraz in regional sports histories.

## Biography

After graduating with a degree in Physical Education from the University of Tehran, Madadi played as a forward for Taj Shiraz, then Taj Tehran (now known as Esteghlal Tehran), and finally Bargh Shiraz.

Following his retirement from professional football, he worked as a physical education instructor and supervisor at Shiraz University of Technology.

## Later life and legacy

Madadi is listed among veteran footballers of Shiraz in regional sports literature. In Tarikh e Varzesh e Fars, he is featured with photographs and identified as a former Taj player and a representative of Shiraz football during the late 1960s and 1970s.

Amiad Water Systems Ltd. (Hebrew: עמיעד מערכות מים בע"מ) is an Israeli company that develops, manufactures, and markets water treatment and filtration systems for the industrial, municipal, and irrigation sectors. The company is publicly traded on the] (TASE: AMD) and is controlled by the private equity firm FIMI Opportunity Funds.

## History

The company was founded in 1962 at Kibbutz Ami'ad in the Upper Galilee, originally operating under the name Amiad Filtration Systems and focusing on filtration for agricultural irrigation.

In 2005, the company was admitted to trading on the Alternative Investment Market (AIM) of the].

In 2010, Amiad acquired Arkal Filtration Systems from Kibbutz] and Bermad, merging two major Israeli filtration entities. The acquisition incorporated Arkal's disc filtration technology (Spin Klin) into Amiad's portfolio.

In 2020, FIMI Opportunity Funds acquired a controlling stake (approx. 46%) in the company, purchasing shares from Kibbutz Amiad and other shareholders. Following the acquisition, in 2021, the company delisted from the London Stock Exchange and registered its shares for trading on the].

In 2023, the company acquired Aslan Technologies, a Canadian water treatment company.

## Technologies and Applications

Amiad manufactures automatic self-cleaning filters using several core technologies:

* **Suction-scanning screens:** Utilizing a focused flush mechanism to clean filtration screens without interrupting flow.

* **Disc filtration (Spin Klin):** Using compressed grooved discs for depth filtration, a technology originally developed by Arkal.

* **Microfiber:** Thread cassettes for fine filtration.

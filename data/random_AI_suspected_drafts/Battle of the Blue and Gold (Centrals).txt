The Battle of the Blue and Gold (also known as the Battle of the Centrals) is an annual cricket match played between Nugawela Central College and Walala A. Ratnayake Central College in the Kandy District, Sri Lanka. The match is played at the Asgiriya Stadium in Kandy.

## History

The cricket encounter was first played in 2004. Both schools share blue and gold as their official school colors, which led to the match being named the "Battle of the Blue and Gold". Although it began as a limited-overs match, it is now played as a two-day fixture.

## Series Summary

As of 2024, Nugawela Central College has won 6 matches, while Walala A. Ratnayake Central College has won 4. Nine matches have ended in draws.

## Notable Performances

* 16th encounter held in 2019: Walala A. Ratnayake Central College won the match by 8 wickets. Dinuka Sarathchandra took 11 wickets in the match.
* 19th encounter held in 2024: Nugawela Central College won by 115 runs. Janidu Shehara scored 75 not out, and Daham Nirmal took 11 wickets for 68 runs.

## Venue

The matches are primarily played at the Asgiriya Stadium, which is the home ground of Trinity College, Kandy. It is one of the few school-owned cricket grounds in the world to have hosted Test matches.

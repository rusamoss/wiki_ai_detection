Real Story Group is an industry analyst firm originally focussed on reviewing content management systems... Founded as CMS Watch, the company has since branched out into related content oriented technologies, and in 2010 changed its name to Real Story Group (RSG) to reflect this change. According to founder Tony Byrne, the firm differentiates itself by working only for technology buyers, maintaining strict software vendor neutrality

In 2017, Rosenfield Media published the book "The Right Way to Select Technology", based on the experience of RSG analysts in helping organisations select technology

Champions Speakers is a British speakers’ bureau that organises keynote speakers, event hosts and corporate entertainers for business and public events. It is part of Champions (UK) plc and is based in Loughborough, United Kingdom.

## History

Champions Speakers was founded in 2003 as part of Champions (UK) plc. In September 2025, the agency acquired SpeakOut, an entertainment and keynote speaker bureau with offices in London and Edinburgh, founded in 1985. The acquisition was reported by business media outlets including *TheBusinessDesk* and *Insider Media*.

## Operations

The agency provides speaker booking services for corporate functions, conferences, award ceremonies and similar events. Its speaker roster includes individuals from sectors such as business leadership, technology, sport and entertainment. Leadership of the bureau is publicly listed with John Hayes serving as Chief Executive and Matthew Hayes serving as Managing Director.

## Recognition

* In June 2025, Champions Speakers won the Made in the UK, Sold to the World Awards in the Consultancy & Professional Services category, a government-administered programme recognising exporters and professional service firms.
* Champions Speakers was listed among the Top 10 UK Specialist Speaker Agencies in a 2025 industry guide distributed by AP News / EIN-Presswire.
* The wider Champions group has previously appeared in national growth rankings, with regional media noting its inclusion in the Sunday Times Fast Track alumni and London Stock Exchange’s 1000 Companies to Inspire Britain.

Nektarios Kyrkopoulos is a Greek model who has worked internationally, particularly in European and Asian markets.

## Early life

Kyrkopoulos was born in Germany to Greek parents and later moved to Greece

## Career

Kyrkopoulos began his modeling career after being discovered by a scout while in Mykonos. He has since developed an international career, working in several countries including China, India and South Korea.

He has appeared in fashion editorials and publications, including features in Esquire magazine. Kyrkopoulos has also participated in international projects in India, including collaborations with Gauri Khan. He has also appeared in editorial fashion work for L’Officiel Hommes Ukraine. Kyrkopoulos has appeared in interviews and segments on Greek television and digital media platforms.

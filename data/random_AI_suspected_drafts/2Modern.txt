## 2Modern

2Modern is an American e-commerce retailer specializing in contemporary furniture, lighting, rugs, outdoor furnishings, and home décor. Founded in 2003 by Greg Finney, the company operates from Mill Valley, California, and offers a curated selection of modern design products sourced from international and emerging designers.

## History

2Modern was founded in 2003 by Greg Finney, whose vision was shaped by a passion for authentic modern design and early recognition of e-commerce opportunities in furniture retail. The company continues to operate independently under its incorporated name, Carve Media, Inc., and remains headquartered in Mill Valley, California.

## Products and Services

2Modern’s offerings span multiple categories including bedroom, living, dining, office, outdoor, tabletop, decor, lighting, and rugs. ## Business Model

As a pure-play online retailer, 2Modern emphasizes curated product selection and authenticity rather than private-label production. The company bolsters customer experience with services such as a 100 % price-match guarantee, 30-day returns, and free shipping on orders over $50.

## Trade Program

2Modern operates a dedicated Trade Program for design professionals, architects, and commercial clients. Trade members receive exclusive trade pricing, personalized support from a dedicated account executive, concierge-level service from planning through installation, and access to interior design services. The program also includes volume discounts and a trade-focused product catalog and line sheets.

## Recognition

2Modern has been featured in design publications such as The New York Times, Architectural Digest, Elle Decor, Dwell, and Better Homes & Gardens. In 2023, it was named a “Retail Star” by Home Accents Today for its role in pioneering modern e-commerce and global sourcing.

## Corporate Profile

According to the Better Business Bureau, 2Modern, Inc. (d/b/a 2Modern.com) is an A+-rated and BBB-accredited company. The incorporation under Carve Media, Inc. traces back to 1997, and Greg Finney is listed as the CEO.

Manix Wong is a Hong Kong-born fashion designer based in Paris, known for his work in menswear and as the founder of the brand LACLOS Paris.

## Early life and education

Wong was born in Hong Kong. He studied fashion design at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, earning a Bachelor of Arts in Fashion Design. He later completed a Master’s degree in menswear at Central Saint Martins in London.

## Career

Wong founded the menswear brand LACLOS Paris in 2004. The brand initially focused on cashmere knitwear.

His work has been presented internationally, including at events associated with Berlin Fashion Week. In 2025, he presented a collection at the Vietnam International Trade Fair (VIATT) in Ho Chi Minh City.

Wong has participated in the Festival International des Créateurs de Mode in Dinard and Dinan, including presentations and invited appearances at various editions of the event.

His work has also appeared in runway presentations and exhibitions, including Texworld in Paris.

A runway presentation featuring Wong’s work at Salon du Chocolat in Paris has been documented in media coverage.

## Awards and recognition

Wong has received several industry awards, including:

* First place at the Smirnoff International Fashion Awards in Hong Kong, and second place at the international finals in Brazil in 1993.
* Premium Young Designers Award, Berlin (2008).
* Grand Prix Créateur de Dinard, Festival International des Jeunes Créateurs de Mode (2010).
* International Design Awards (2022), including two gold awards and one bronze award.

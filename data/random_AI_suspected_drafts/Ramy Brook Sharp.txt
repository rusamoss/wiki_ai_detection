Ramy Brook Sharp is an American fashion designer and entrepreneur. She is the founder and creative director of the women's contemporary fashion label Ramy Brook, which she launched in 2010.

## Early life

Sharp grew up in New Jersey and attended Chatham High School. In interviews, she has described developing an early interest in clothing and design, including altering garments at a young age.

Before entering the fashion industry professionally, Sharp worked in advertising, an experience she has cited as part of her broader professional background prior to launching her own label.

## Career

Sharp founded Ramy Brook in 2010. Media profiles have described the brand’s origins as deliberately small, noting that Sharp initially developed a limited collection consisting of six tops and one dress. Early sales took place through trunk shows and personal networks, including an early trunk show hosted at her sister’s home in Short Hills, New Jersey.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Sharp discussed the company’s increased focus on direct-to-consumer and e-commerce strategies in interviews with fashion industry publications.

## Philanthropy and recognition

Sharp has been publicly recognized in connection with philanthropic and community-focused initiatives. In 2016, she was named an honoree associated with City of Hope’s Spirit of Life luncheon through the organization’s East End Chapter.

Later in 2016, she was also recognized at the New York Renaissance Basketball Association (RENS) gala for her involvement with youth and community initiatives.

In 2018, Sharp was honored at the Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation’s Hamptons Happening benefit, with coverage noting her involvement in cancer research fundraising efforts.

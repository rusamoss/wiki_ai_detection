Bellissimo Pizza is a chain of pizzerias headquartered in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. A 2023 profile published by Spot.uz described the company’s growth to 39 branches by May 2023.

## History

Spot.uz published a company profile in 2023 describing its early development and operations in several Uzbek cities.

## Rebranding

In 2024, Sostav and the trade publication Pizza Marketplace reported that the company introduced an updated visual identity created by the branding agency Suprematika.

## Expansion to Kazakhstan

In 2025, Spot.uz reported that the company planned openings in Astana. DigitalBusiness.kz later published an interview about the company’s entry into the Kazakh market following the opening of a first location in Astana.

In November 2025, Spot.uz reported that the chain acquired six former Papa John's locations in Almaty for conversion to the Bellissimo Pizza brand. Kursiv also mentioned the planned conversion while reporting on Papa John’s departure from Central Asia.

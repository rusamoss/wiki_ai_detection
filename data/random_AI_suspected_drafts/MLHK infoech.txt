## Overview

MLHK infotech is an Indian information technology services and consulting company founded in 2020, based in Shajapur district, Madhya Pradesh. The company specializes in digital transformation services, web development, mobile application development, and related IT solutions.

## History and Founding

MLHK infotech was established in 2020 and is headquartered in Barnawad, Shajapur, Madhya Pradesh, India. The company was founded by Hariom Vishwakarma, who serves as Founder and CEO. The company's motto is "Make Digital Life Easy."

## Services

MLHK infotech provides a range of information technology services including:

* Website development and design
* Mobile application development
* Web design services
* IT consulting
* Digital marketing services
* IT solutions

' Shyamoli Paribahan Private Limited'

Shyamoli Paribahan Private Limited is an Indian intercity bus operator headquartered in Kolkata, West Bengal. Established in 2004, the company operates long-distance and interstate bus services across multiple states in India. It provides sleeper and seater coach services using a fleet that includes multi-axle buses manufactured by companies such as Volvo, Bharat Benz, Ashok Leyland, and Scania bus coaches

' History'

Shyamoli Paribahan Private Limited was founded in 2004 by Mr.Arun Kumar Ghosh. The company initially operated a single route between Kolkata and Asansol. Over time, it expanded its network to include interstate routes connecting cities in eastern and southern India.

During the 2010s, the company introduced air-conditioned sleeper and semi-sleeper coaches and implemented online ticket booking and GPS-based vehicle tracking systems.

As of 2025, Shyamoli Paribahan Private Limited operates a fleet of more than 100 buses across several Indian states, including West Bengal, Odisha, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Jharkhand, Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Maharashtra.

The company offers scheduled intercity services with amenities that may include charging points, onboard entertainment systems, and live vehicle tracking. Online ticket booking is available through its official website and mobile application.

' Network'

Shyamoli Paribahan Private Limited operates routes connecting Kolkata, Bhubaneswar, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kochi, Mumbai, and Pune. The company also serves regional towns across eastern and southern India.

Kasawoo is an architecture and design practice based in London founded in 2020 by Katie Kasabalis and Darius Woo. The practice was included in Wallpaper* magazine's Architects' Directory 2024, which spotlights emerging architecture studios around the world, and was included among 93 UK practices in the Architecture Foundation 's New Architects 5 lineup in 2025.

## Works

The Root, a prefabricated holiday cabin on Zakynthos, Greece was featured as the cover story of Dwell magazine's Jan/Feb 2026 ‘The Generations Issue’, with over 200,000 copies circulated worldwide. Subsequently, the project was also published as the cover story of Enki magazine's volume 73 'The Holiday Issue'. In addition, the project has also been featured in digital media outlets such as Dezeen, ArchDaily, The Greek Foundation and Architectural Digest (France).

## Recognition

In 2024, the practice's work was shortlisted in the spatial category of the FRAME Awards.

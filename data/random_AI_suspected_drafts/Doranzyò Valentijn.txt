Doranzyò A.J. Valentijn (12 November 2003) is a Dutch entrepreneur and politician. In 2020 he started his political career as a vocal critic of the Dutch government’s decisions regarding COVID-19 and through voluntary work for the right-wing nationalist party Forum for Democracy. In 2022, he became a regional coordinator for the party, and in 2026 he represented the party in the Rucphen municipal council as a member of the Social Affairs Committee.

## Early life

As a child, Valentijn always performed well at school, but at the start of secondary school, he was taken away from his parents due to the corruption of the Dutch youth care system. His life was turned upside down, and it took until he was 16 years old before he was allowed to live with his father again. The corruption of the Dutch youth care system that affected him and his family, along with the restrictive government measures surrounding COVID-19, ultimately led him to take action to reform the youth care system, so that no child would have to go through what his family had been subjected to.

## Christianity

Born in Sint Willebrord, a Catholic town in North Brabant, Valentijn was raised in a culturally Catholic family. After researching the theology and history of the Abrahamic religions (Christianity, Islam, and Judaism) since 2020, he became Traditional Catholic in 2025.

This contributed to Valentijn’s political position regarding mass immigration and Dutch culture.

## Political career

In 2020, at the age of 16, Valentijn began his political career by volunteering for the Dutch right-wing nationalist party Forum for Democracy and joining its youth organisation. In June 2021, he took a further step towards a political career, becoming the party group assistant to Breda ’s local party leader, Marnix van Woudenberg. In October 2022, he also became a regional coordinator, covering his home municipality of Rucphen. In this role, he coordinated a volunteer group, organised political events, and oversaw the coordination of the local, provincial, and national election campaigns. This period ended in March 2025.

In February 2026, at the age of 22, he appeared on the electoral list for the municipal elections in Rucphen. In April 2026, he was appointed by local party leader Brigitte Clercx to fulfill the role of committee member on the Social Affairs Committee in April 2026.

## Business career

In September 2024, Valentijn officially registered his company DutchMadeEasy where he teaches Dutch to non-native speakers through the Discord community “ DutchMadeEasy ”.

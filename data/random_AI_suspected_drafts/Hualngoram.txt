Hualngoram (historically called Hualngo Land) is a cultural and geographic region in the western part of Chin State, Myanmar, along the international border with the Indian state of Mizoram. The area is inhabited primarily by the Hualngo people. Other ethnic groups and tribes also reside in the region, including the Zahau, Tlaisun, and Zaniat, among others.

Hualngoram is known for its ethnic identity, hilly terrain, and its strategic border location that has shaped both its history and modern challenges. Mid-2025 saw increased attention toward the region following clashes between rebel groups, the Chin Defense Force-Hualngoram (CDF-Hualngoram) and the Chin National Defence Force (CNDF). Local inhabitants sought refuge in Mizoram, India, returning only after the CNDF had retreated.

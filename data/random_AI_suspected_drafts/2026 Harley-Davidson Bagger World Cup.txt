The 2026 Harley-Davidson Bagger World Cup season is the inaugural season of the Harley-Davidson Bagger World Cup motorcycle racing series. The competition is held as a support category during selected MotoGP events in North America and Europe. The championship features race-prepared Harley-Davidson touring motorcycles and marks the first time a dedicated bagger motorcycle series appears on the MotoGP support schedule.

## Teams and riders

Four teams were confirmed during the pre-season announcement, with full rider line-ups scheduled for release before the opening round.

## Race weekend format

Most rounds follow a two-day structure:

* Friday: Free Practice 1 (FP1) and Free Practice 2 (FP2)
* Saturday: Qualifying and Race 1
* Sunday: Race 2

The Mugello round uses an adjusted format:

* Friday: FP1, FP2, and Qualifying
* Saturday: Race 1 and Race 2

## Bikes

Bikes used in the series are based on Harley-Davidson’s Grand American Touring platform and adapted for closed-course racing. The motorcycles are race-prepared versions of the Road Glide model, modified under a technical package provided by Harley-Davidson Factory Racing.

Further technical regulations are expected to be released before the first round.

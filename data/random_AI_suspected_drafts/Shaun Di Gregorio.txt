Shaun Di Gregorio is an Australian business executive and entrepreneur known for his involvement in online marketplaces and digital classifieds. He is the founder and former chief executive officer of Frontier Digital Ventures Ltd, an Australian Securities Exchange–listed company that invests in and operates online classified advertising businesses in emerging markets. Di Gregorio has also served as chief executive officer of iProperty Group and held senior leadership roles at REA Group.

## Early life and education

Executive profiles indicate that he earned a Master of Business Administration from the Australian Graduate School of Management.

## Career

### REA Group

Di Gregorio held senior management roles at REA Group, one of Australia's largest digital property companies. Business press coverage reports that he contributed to the development and expansion of REA Group's online classifieds platforms during a period of significant growth for the company.

### iProperty Group

He later became chief executive officer of iProperty Group, an Asia-focused online property classifieds business. As CEO, Di Gregorio oversaw regional expansion and operational scaling across multiple Asian markets. Media coverage noted that the company grew to more than 300 employees during this period prior to its acquisition by REA Group.

### Frontier Digital Ventures

In 2014, Di Gregorio co-founded Frontier Digital Ventures Ltd (ASX: FDV), a publicly listed company specialising in online classifieds businesses in emerging markets, particularly in property and automotive verticals. Under his leadership, the company pursued growth through minority and majority investments across Asia, Latin America, and North Africa.

In 2020, Frontier Digital Ventures undertook a $100 million equity raise to support further acquisitions and portfolio expansion.

In a 2024 interview, Di Gregorio stated that Frontier Digital Ventures had achieved sustained positive operating cash flow across its portfolio of marketplace businesses.

In 2025, Frontier Digital Ventures announced that Di Gregorio would step down as chief executive officer and transition to a non-executive director role after leading the company since its founding.

## Industry recognition

Di Gregorio has been profiled by independent technology, media, and investment publications for his work in developing online marketplace businesses in emerging economies. His investment approach and leadership have been discussed in interviews with *Disrupt Africa* and Australian financial media outlets.

Red Wagon Growth Marketing Agency (also known as Red Wagon Agency) is an American digital marketing firm headquartered in Tempe, Arizona. The agency was founded on June 15, 2015, by Joe Whyte, a digital marketing practitioner with prior experience in paid media, search engine optimization, and media buying. It is registered as a sole proprietorship and holds an A+ rating from the Better Business Bureau.

## History

Red Wagon Agency was established in June 2015 in Tempe, Arizona. Its founder, Joe Whyte, had previously operated HybridSEM, a digital marketing firm active from 2006 to 2008 that was subsequently acquired by TextLinkBrokers.com. Following that acquisition, Whyte served as Director of Emerging Media at TextLinkBrokers through 2010. He later held the title of Marketing Scientist at Colling Media, a Phoenix-based advertising agency, before founding Red Wagon.

## Services

Red Wagon Agency provides digital marketing services including pay-per-click advertising, search engine optimization, email marketing, content marketing, social media management, media buying, CRM integration, and website development. The agency also operates an outsource program through which other marketing firms may contract its services.

## Tra-digital advertising

A methodology associated with Red Wagon Agency and its founder is tra-digital advertising, a term attributed to Whyte to describe the integration of traditional broadcast media — including television, radio, outdoor, and print — with digital tracking infrastructure and paid search optimization.

The approach was described in a 2017 article in Street Fight Magazine by Mindy Weinstein, which outlined four methods Whyte associated with bridging traditional and digital media attribution:

* Monitoring branded search volume in Google Analytics as an indicator of traditional media exposure.
* Using unique vanity URLs in traditional advertisements to enable digital tracking.
* Assigning distinct telephone numbers to individual traditional media placements for call attribution.
* Deploying Google Ads bid adjustment scripts to increase paid search bids during the scheduled broadcast times of television and radio advertisements.

The term was subsequently used as a brand identifier by both Colling Media and Red Wagon Agency, and as the name of an earlier version of Whyte's podcast.

## Client work

According to the agency's published case studies, Red Wagon has worked with clients across industries including software-as-a-service, retail, legal services, precious metals, and pharmacy. Engagements have included paid media management, marketing technology implementation, CRM integration, and attribution system development.

## Publications

Whyte authored The Ultimate Guide to Google Ads, published in December 2020, along with two additional books on digital marketing topics. The agency produces a podcast titled Marketing Master Class, hosted by Whyte, covering paid media strategy and demand generation. A prior version of the podcast was titled Tra-Digital Academy.

CentroMotion is an American privately held industrial manufacturing company that designs and produces motion, actuation, and control systems used in industrial and transportation applications. The company is headquartered in Waukesha, Wisconsin.

Founded in 2019, CentroMotion operates a portfolio of engineering and manufacturing brands supplying components for off‑highway equipment, commercial vehicles, and industrial machinery.

## History

CentroMotion was established in October 2019 following the acquisition of the Engineered Components & Systems segment of Enerpac Tool Group (formerly Actuant Corporation) by One Rock Capital Partners. The business was subsequently renamed CentroMotion and positioned as an independent company serving industrial and off‑highway equipment markets.

The original business included established brands such as CrossControl, Elliott Manufacturing, Gits Manufacturing, maximatecc, Power‑Packer, and Weasler Engineering.

In May 2021, CentroMotion announced an agreement to acquire Carlisle Brake & Friction from Carlisle Companies. The acquisition was completed in August 2021, expanding the company’s capabilities in braking systems and friction materials for off‑highway equipment.

In June 2023, One Rock Capital Partners entered into an agreement to sell CentroMotion to affiliates of Lone Star Funds. The transaction was completed in September 2023.

## Operations

CentroMotion develops engineered systems and components for industrial and transportation applications. Its products are primarily supplied to original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and aftermarket channels.

The company’s core areas of activity include:

* Hydraulic actuation and motion control systems
* Mechanical power transmission and driveline components
* Human–machine interface (HMI) technologies
* Engine thermal and emission control systems
* Industrial braking systems and friction materials

CentroMotion operates manufacturing and engineering facilities globally, including locations in North America and Europe.

## Brands

CentroMotion operates a multi‑brand structure consisting of established industrial manufacturers, including:

* Carlisle Brake & Friction
* CrossControl
* Elliott Manufacturing
* Gits Manufacturing
* maximatecc
* Power‑Packer
* Weasler Engineering

These brands supply components and systems used in sectors such as agriculture, construction, mining, and transportation.

## Headquarters

CentroMotion is headquartered in Waukesha, Wisconsin. The company relocated operations within southeastern Wisconsin following its formation.

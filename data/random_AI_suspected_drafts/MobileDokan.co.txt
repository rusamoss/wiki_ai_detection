MobileDokan.co is a popular mobile information platform founded in 2019 by Sourav Sarkar. With its reliable data, simple presentation, and frequent updates, it has earned a strong position in Bangladesh as a trusted source for smartphone specifications and tech-related information. It is essentially an information-based online platform where users can make necessary comparisons or research before purchasing a mobile phone.

## History

MobileDokan.co was founded in 2019. The domain was registered in February of the same year, and the website officially went live shortly afterward. From the very beginning, its mission has been to deliver accurate, reliable, and up-to-date smartphone information to Bangladeshi users.

Although the site initially launched with a relatively small amount of data, it quickly expanded and eventually became one of the most popular mobile information websites in Bangladesh.

## Features

* The platform focuses on:
* Full smartphone specifications
* Official and unofficial prices in Bangladesh
* Reviews, analysis, and expert opinions
* High-resolution images and hands-on gallery
* Latest news and availability updates
* User comments and ratings
* Device comparison tools

MobileDokan.co gathers data from multiple trusted sources, verifies the information, and then publishes it. Users are also encouraged to report inaccurate information so the site can maintain the highest level of accuracy.

In the future, the team plans to introduce mobile phone sales and home delivery services — potentially transforming the site from an information platform into a full e-commerce service.

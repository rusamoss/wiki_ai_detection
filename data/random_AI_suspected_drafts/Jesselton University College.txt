# Jesselton University College

## Introduction

Jesselton University College (JUC) is a private higher education institution located in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia. It was established in 1988 under the Sabah Education Ordinance 1961 as Sabah Institute Management. Following the enactment of the Private Higher Educational Institutions Act 1996 (Malaysia), the institution was re-registered as Institut Prima Bestari in 1999. It was later renamed to Jesselton College in 2008. It was granted university college status in 2021 by the Ministry of Higher Education (Malaysia) and renaming to Jesselton University College.

Faculties:

* School of Business and Law

* School of Education and Psychology

* Centre of Postgraduate Studies

* Jesselton English Institute

* Department of Skills Development

## Academic Profile and Quality Recognition

Jesselton University College's academic programmes follows Malaysian Qualifications Agency (MQA) and are recognised by the Ministry of Human Resources (Malaysia). The institution is a licensed training provider for the Human Resource Development Corporation (HRD Corp), and the Department of Skills Development (Jabatan Pembangunan Kemahiran, JPK).

## Rankings

The '''1999 Generali Ladies Linz''' was a women's professional tennis tournament played in [[Linz]], Austria. It was part of the [[1999 WTA Tour]] and was classified as a Tier II event. The tournament was held indoors at the Intersport Arena from 25 to 31 October 1999.

[[Mary Pierce]] won the singles title, defeating fellow French player [[Sandrine Testud]] in the final. The doubles title was won by [[Barbara Schett]] and [[Patty Schnyder]], who defeated [[Alexandra Fusai]] and [[Nathalie Tauziat]].

== Tournament details ==

The event was one of the late-season indoor tournaments on the WTA Tour calendar. As a Tier II tournament, it attracted several highly ranked players and served as an important stop in the closing weeks of the 1999 season. The singles draw consisted of 28 players, while the doubles competition featured 16 teams.

Pierce's victory in the singles final came against Testud in an all-French championship match. In doubles, the Austrian-Swiss pairing of Schett and Schnyder won the title, giving Schett a tournament victory on home soil.

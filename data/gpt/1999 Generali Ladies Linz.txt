1999 Generali Ladies Linz was a women's tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts at the Intersport Arena in Linz, Austria. It was the 13th edition of the Generali Ladies Linz and was part of the Tier II category of the 1999 WTA Tour. The tournament was held from 18 to 24 October 1999.

Mary Pierce won the singles title, while Alexandra Fusai and Nathalie Tauziat won the doubles title.

## Finals

### Singles

Mary Pierce defeated Barbara Schett, 6–4, 6–3.

### Doubles

Alexandra Fusai and Nathalie Tauziat won the doubles title.
